Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting your virtual office.

Is using a virtual office for GST registration legal in India?›
Yes, completely legal. The GST department accepts virtual office addresses provided the correct documentation is in place — a notarized rent agreement, NOC from the property owner, and a utility bill. We provide all three with every plan.
Can I register a new company at a virtual office address?›
Yes. Our Business plan includes all documents required by MCA for company incorporation — rent agreement, NOC, and address proof. These are accepted by the Registrar of Companies across all states.
What documents do I get with my virtual office?›
You receive: (1) Notarized Rent/Lease Agreement, (2) NOC from property owner, (3) Utility bill copy, (4) Address proof letter. All delivered digitally within 24–48 hours.
How long does setup take?›
Most setups complete within 3–5 working days from payment and KYC submission. Express 24-hour delivery is available in many cities.
Will my courier and business mail be handled?›
Yes. From the GST and Business plans, your physical mail and couriers are received, logged, and scanned. You get real-time WhatsApp/email notifications. Forwarding is available as an add-on.
How much does a virtual office cost?›
Plans start at ₹699/month for mailing, ₹1,099/month for GST-ready, and ₹1,499/month for full company + GST setup. All billed annually. No hidden fees.
Will the GST officer physically inspect the address?›
Physical inspections are rare. Our partner locations have trained staff who handle GST officer visits — verifying registration and presenting documentation. Included in all plans.
Can I get GST registration in multiple states?›
Absolutely. We offer multi-state bundles at discounted rates. Contact us for a custom quote if you need 3 or more states.
Is there a long-term lock-in or cancellation fee?›
No lock-in, no cancellation fee. Plans are annual with reminders 30 days before expiry. If we fail to deliver on time, we offer a full refund.
Do Amazon / Flipkart / marketplace sellers accept virtual offices?›
Yes. Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, and other marketplaces accept virtual office addresses for seller verification, provided you have valid GST registration documents.
What if my GST application is rejected?›
We re-prepare and resubmit at no cost. Our first-time approval rate is over 98%. If unresolved, we offer a full refund.
Can I visit the office or hold meetings there?›
Our Business plan includes meeting room access (2 hours/month) at partner locations. Additional hours available on request.
